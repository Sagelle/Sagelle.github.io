About Elle Zeka

Elle Zeka is a creative studio I founded to help scholars, founders, faith communities, and brands tell their story beautifully. Born from a love of design and a belief that good visuals can carry serious ideas, the studio works across branding, graphic design, and digital content. Recent work includes the 2026 SANS Symposium banner artwork, the LGFMI Magazine, and design and editing for the Laugh 2 Compassion Gala.
