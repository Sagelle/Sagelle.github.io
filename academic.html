Research interests

The questions I'm asking

①

Neural circuits & behaviour

Understanding how the brain encodes, adapts, and produces behaviour using electrophysiological tools.

②

Molecular neuroscience

Single-nucleus RNA sequencing to characterise cellular diversity and disease-relevant mechanisms in the brain.

③

Translational neuroscience

Bridging basic science to clinical impact via TMS, MEG, and human participant research.

④

Apathy & esketamine

My current research investigates the neural and behavioural underpinnings of apathy — the loss of motivation that affects millions living with depression and neurodegenerative disease — and explores how esketamine, a fast-acting antidepressant, can restore the drive to act.
